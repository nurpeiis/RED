Get to know us


Meaningful Connections

Dedicated to upholding the university's commitment to facilitating a center for scholarship and innovation, the Office of Community and Education Engagement seeks to develop professional connections that are mutually beneficial for both the university and the client. The office aims to establish ties between the university and companies, NGOs and government organizations from a plethora of fields.



Innovation

We aim to build connections that promote technological and social innovation. Whether in the form of engaging with partners to develop programs that teach students how to use technology or working with multinational research organizations, the office is prepared to connect partners and the university to ensure the establishment of these relationships



Education

We believe in the vital role that education plays in shaping communities and the world. Dedicated to upholding the values upon which NYU Abu Dhabi was founded upon, valuing a diversity of opinion and universal dialogue, the office engages with partners to create programs that benefit students and faculty from NYU Abu Dhabi, universities around the United Arab Emirates, as well as students and faculty from secondary and primary education centers.




Community

We believe in the importance of NYU Abu Dhabi's continued integration into the wider Emirati community. The office will continue to develop relationships with important organizations, companies and agencies across the nation as the university and the United Arab Emirates continue to grow together.
