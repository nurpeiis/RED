RED News


CoLab and DCT

Dedicated to upholding the university's commitment to facilitating a center On November 4, the Office of Community and Education Engagement held its first Roadmap Engagement Design (RED) workshop in collaboration with the Department of Culture and Tourism. The workshop’s aim was to visualize, develop and present potential projects that NYU Abu Dhabi could implement with the aide of its external partners. The points of collaboration were divided into seven fields: HR/Internships, Innovation, Marketing & Branding, Heritage & Culture, Arts & Music, Tourism and the Library, in accordance with RED’s target fields for development. It was a productive afternoon that allowed for faculty, students and our external partners to meet and promote engagement on future projects.
